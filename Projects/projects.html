My Hobby Projects:

Most of my projects do not stay put together, as my storage space is very limited, and the practicality of these projects is also limited. Nevertheless, here are a few that I have because I use them constantly or they are recent enough for me to have pictures of:

A handheld Raspberry Pi system, designed and modeled in CATIA. You can learn more about this project here.

This is a large RC airplane with glider-like wings spanning 5 feet (1.5 meters). This airplane I put together to carry a camera or similar payload for a longer flight. I constructed the airframe out of foam board, tape and hot glue. The airframe design was based on a Flite Test schematic, but altered slightly to fit my electronics components.

A much smaller triplane design I built to fly around for fun. This airplane I fly frequently and has exceptional low-speed control due to the three-wing design.

My current working computer. I built it with off-the-shelf components and some spare parts I had. It isn't cutting-edge, but it does what I need it to.
