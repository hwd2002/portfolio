Harrison's Portfolio

    My name is Harrison Davis. I am a programmer and an aspiring Mechanical Engineer attending college in North Carolina. In my free time, I program small games, simulators, and AI systems. I also enjoy making RC vehicles of all types to achieve my self-set goals.

    Some of my projects are viewable on this website (including this website itself!) You can use the sidebar to navigate through the different categories.

My Programming Experiments and Programs

    I program small games, demos, or simulations to experiment and learn about new concepts such as Neural Nets, PID loops, Matrix Multiplication, and Perspective Projection. To make this website, I learned HTML and CSS.

My Artistic Endeavors

    Apart from the graphical artwork I do for my games and other programming projects, I sketch aircraft with pencil and use Photoshop for more complicated works. I have also used charcoal from time to time. I do not consider myself an artist however I do sketch aircraft, vehicles, and concept art when I feel like it.

My Projects

    I frequently build PCs for myself or family members. Some of these are brand-new, while others are scrapped together from parts. I also build and fly homemade Radio Controlled aircraft as a hobby.
